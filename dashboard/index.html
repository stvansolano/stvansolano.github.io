FAQ

  1. What should I bring?
    • We encourage you to bring your personal computer to you local Xamarin Dev Days event with Xamarin fully installed to participate in the mini-hacks and live coding activities. We have a full guide for both Mac OS X and Windows to help get you started.
    • Xamarin experts will be around to help you with any setup that you may need.
  2. Will food be provided?
    • At each Dev Days event there will be a complimentary breakfast and coffee served in the morning before the event. Halfway through the day a complimentary lunch will be served before we start our mini-hacks, labs, and ask the experts. Food and beverages are determined on a venue-by-venue- basis, but feel free to contact us with any questions.
  3. Can my company sponsor Xamarin Dev Days?
    • Sponsorship opportunities are available for either a single event or for multiple Xamarin Dev Days events. Feel free to contact devdays@xamarin.com or the local organizers to discuss details.
  4. How do I get there?
    • TELUS Garden: 2a3, 575 W Georgia St, Vancouver, BC V6B 2A3, Canada
